College Apartments. Simplified.

My best friends Can and Cem and I sought to improve the college hunting experience after having gone through a pretty wild couple of weeks searching for a place to stay. Habito actively matches student preferences with available apartment listings found on Craigslist, obtained through the 3taps API.

Click here to see a test site of the available listings near Berkeley.

This site was built using Django and is hosted on Heroku.

The most refreshing power nap ever.

The alarm begins when you fall asleep, making sure that a 25 minute power nap is actually a 25 minute power nap.

The app uses the accelerometer and is calibrated to the phone's initial position. After a period of insignificant movement, the app will determine that the user has fallen asleep and set an alarm to wake the user up.

Download the app here on the Google Play Store.
